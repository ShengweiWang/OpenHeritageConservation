Project Description

The Alamo conservation project is developing technology to support digital recordkeeping of the conservation efforts underway at the Alamo. We are developing a Web-based conservation management system based on the LAMP architecture and open conservation standards, such as CIDOC-CRM. Traditionally, these records have been maintained on paper. We aim to share the recent discoveries made at Shrine of Texas with a broad audience. The project will initially develop technology to support the capture of conservation data using 3-D models and a visual Web interface to conservation data. In the next phase, we aim to develop educational and information apps to enhance the experience of students and visitors to the Alamo.
